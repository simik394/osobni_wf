Here is a list of camping gear essentials that you may need for your weekend camping trip:
  • Tent: This is where you will sleep, so make sure it is big enough for everyone and that it is in good condition.
  • Sleeping bags: These will keep you warm at night. Choose sleeping bags that are rated for the temperature range you will be camping in.
  • Sleeping pads: These will add extra insulation and comfort to your sleeping bags.
  • Camp chairs: These will be a lifesaver when you are relaxing around the campfire or just taking a break from hiking
